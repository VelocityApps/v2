## Top Competitors

### Smile.io (Now Yotpo Loyalty)
**Users:** 100,000+
**Pricing:** £49 - £249/month
**Revenue Estimate:** ~£60M ARR

**What They Solve:**
- Points-based loyalty program
- Referral program
- VIP tiers
- Rewards and discounts
- Loyalty analytics

**What They Do RIGHT:**
✅ **Massive adoption** - 100k users (proven model)
✅ **Points system** - Simple, effective points-based program
✅ **Referral program** - Built-in referral system
✅ **Good analytics** - Shows loyalty program performance
✅ **Strong brand** - Dominates loyalty market

**What They Do WRONG:**
❌ **Expensive** - Starting at £49/month (high entry point)
❌ **Generic program** - Same program for everyone (not personalized)
❌ **No AI optimization** - Can't optimize rewards automatically
❌ **Manual configuration** - Must configure program manually
❌ **Limited free tier** - No free plan

**Our Opportunity:**

**Automation: AI-Powered Loyalty Optimizer**
- **Problem:** Smile.io is expensive, generic, no optimization
- **Solution:** AI optimizes loyalty program automatically (rewards, tiers, etc.)
