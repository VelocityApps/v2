# Customer Support Apps - Market Analysis

## Market Overview
- **TAM:** £800M+/year (every store needs customer support)
- **Category size:** 60+ apps
- **Average pricing:** £15-199/month
- **Market leaders:** Gorgias (15k users), Zendesk (100k users), Re:amaze (10k users)
- **Growth:** 18%/year (customer support becoming more important)

## Top Competitors

### Gorgias
**Users:** 15,000+
**Pricing:** £50 - £750/month
**Revenue Estimate:** ~£25M ARR

**What They Solve:**
- Multi-channel support (email, chat, social)
- Helpdesk management
- Customer service automation
- Order management integration
- Support analytics

**What They Do RIGHT:**
✅ **Shopify-native** - Built specifically for Shopify
✅ **Multi-channel** - Email, chat, social media in one place
✅ **Order integration** - See orders directly in support tickets
✅ **Good automation** - Automated responses and workflows
✅ **Strong brand** - Popular choice for Shopify stores

**What They Do WRONG:**
❌ **Expensive** - Starting at £50/month (high entry point)
❌ **Complex** - Requires setup and training
❌ **No AI responses** - Automated responses are templates (not AI)
❌ **Manual workflows** - Must build workflows manually
❌ **Limited free tier** - No free plan

**Our Opportunity:**

**Automation: AI-Powered Customer Support**
- **Problem:** Gorgias is expensive, complex, template-based responses
- **Solution:** AI generates personalized responses automatically
- **Pricing:** £49/month
