# Inventory Management Apps - Market Analysis

## Market Overview
- **TAM:** £1.2B+/year (every store needs inventory management)
- **Category size:** 80+ apps
- **Average pricing:** £25-299/month
- **Market leaders:** TradeGecko (acquired by QuickBooks), Stocky (built by Shopify), Back in Stock (30k users)
- **Growth:** 18%/year (inventory complexity increasing)

## Top Competitors

### Stocky (Shopify Built-in)
**Users:** 50,000+ (built into Shopify Plus)
**Pricing:** Free (Shopify Plus only)
**Revenue Estimate:** N/A (Shopify internal tool)

**What They Solve:**
- Inventory tracking
- Low stock alerts
- Purchase order management
- Inventory reports
- Multi-location inventory

**What They Do RIGHT:**
✅ **Free for Shopify Plus** - Built into Shopify Plus (no extra cost)
✅ **Native integration** - Works seamlessly with Shopify
✅ **Multi-location** - Handles multiple warehouses
✅ **Purchase orders** - Create POs directly from app
✅ **Good reporting** - Comprehensive inventory reports

**What They Do WRONG:**
❌ **Shopify Plus only** - Not available for regular Shopify stores (£2,300/month Shopify Plus)
❌ **Basic alerts** - Simple low stock alerts (not intelligent)
❌ **No predictive analytics** - Can't predict when you'll run out
❌ **No multi-platform sync** - Only works on Shopify (not Etsy, Amazon, etc.)
❌ **Limited automation** - Basic alerts, not proactive
❌ **No reorder automation** - Must manually create purchase orders

**What They DON'T Solve:**
❌ **Predictive inventory** - Can't predict stockout dates
❌ **Multi-platform sync** - Only Shopify, not other platforms
❌ **Auto-reorder** - Must manually reorder stock
❌ **Demand forecasting** - Can't forecast demand
❌ **Regular Shopify stores** - Only Shopify Plus (excludes 95% of stores)

**Our Opportunity:**

**Automation: Predictive Low Stock Alerts**
- **Problem:** Stocky shows alerts when already low (too late), Plus-only
- **Solution:** AI predicts when you'll run out (proactive alerts, works on all Shopify plans)
- **Features:** Demand forecasting, predictive alerts, multi-channel alerts (Slack/email/SMS)
- **Pricing:** £29/month
- **Differentiation:** "Predict stockouts 2 weeks early, not 2 days late"
