# Email Marketing Apps - Market Analysis

## Market Overview
- **TAM:** £2B+/year (every store needs email marketing)
- **Category size:** 100+ apps
- **Average pricing:** £20-299/month
- **Market leaders:** Klaviyo (265k users), Omnisend (80k users), Mailchimp (200k users)
- **Growth:** 25%/year (email marketing ROI continues to improve)

❌ **Manual flow building** - Every store must build flows from scratch (tedious)
❌ **No pre-built templates** - Can't just "turn on" abandoned cart recovery (must build it)
❌ **Steep learning curve** - Needs dedicated email marketing specialist
❌ **Generic templates** - Flow templates exist but not personalized

❌ **Pre-built, optimized flows** - Must build everything manually
❌ **AI-powered personalization** - Segmentation exists but no AI learning
❌ **Send-time optimization** - No AI to determine optimal send time per customer
❌ **Subject line optimization** - No A/B testing or AI suggestions
