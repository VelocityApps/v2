# SEO Optimization Apps - Market Analysis

## Market Overview
- **TAM:** £600M+/year (every store needs SEO)
- **Category size:** 70+ apps
- **Average pricing:** £15-149/month
- **Market leaders:** Plug in SEO (40k users), Smart SEO (20k users), Booster (30k users)
- **Growth:** 22%/year (SEO increasingly important for organic traffic)

## Top Competitors

### Plug in SEO
**Users:** 40,000+
**Pricing:** £19.99 - £99.99/month
**Revenue Estimate:** ~£20M ARR

**What They Solve:**
- SEO audit and analysis
- Meta descriptions optimization
- Alt text for images
- SEO recommendations
- Structured data markup
- Sitemap generation

**What They Do RIGHT:**
✅ **Comprehensive SEO audit** - Identifies all SEO issues
✅ **Good recommendations** - Clear suggestions for improvement
✅ **Easy to use** - Simple interface, good UX
✅ **Affordable** - Starting at £19.99/month
✅ **Good documentation** - Clear guides and tutorials

**What They Do WRONG:**
❌ **Reports problems, doesn't fix them** - Shows issues but doesn't fix automatically
❌ **Manual fixes required** - Must manually fix each issue (tedious)
❌ **Generic recommendations** - Same suggestions for everyone
❌ **No AI optimization** - Static recommendations, no learning
❌ **Slow updates** - Takes time to fix issues manually

**What They DON'T Solve:**
❌ **Automatic fixes** - Can't automatically fix SEO issues
❌ **AI-powered optimization** - No AI to optimize content
❌ **Bulk fixes** - Must fix issues one by one
❌ **Continuous optimization** - One-time audit, not ongoing

**Our Opportunity:**

**Automation: Auto-SEO Fixer**
- **Problem:** Plug in SEO reports problems but doesn't fix them (manual work)
- **Solution:** AI automatically fixes SEO issues (meta descriptions, alt text, etc.)
- **Features:** Auto-generates meta descriptions, alt text, structured data, bulk fixes
- **Pricing:** £35/month
- **Differentiation:** "Plug in SEO reports problems, we fix them automatically"
