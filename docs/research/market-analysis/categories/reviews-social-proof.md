# Reviews & Social Proof Apps - Market Analysis

## Market Overview
- **TAM:** £500M+/year (every store needs reviews)
- **Category size:** 50+ apps
- **Average pricing:** £15-99/month
- **Market leaders:** Judge.me (38k users), Loox (20k users), Stamped.io (50k users)
- **Growth:** 15%/year (reviews increasingly important for conversions)

## Top Competitors

### Judge.me
**Users:** 38,000+
**Pricing:** Free - £15/month
**Revenue Estimate:** ~£1M ARR
**Founded:** 2015
**Team size:** 20-30 employees

**What They Solve:**
- Collect product reviews from customers
- Display reviews on product pages with photos/videos
- Q&A functionality for pre-purchase questions
- Google Shopping integration (show reviews in search results)
- Email automation for review requests
- Review carousels and widgets

**What They Do RIGHT:**
✅ **Generous free tier** - Unlimited reviews on free plan drives massive adoption (38k users!)
✅ **Works with all themes** - Universal compatibility, no theme conflicts
✅ **Simple setup** - 3-step installation process takes <10 minutes
✅ **Google Shopping integration** - Reviews show in Google search results (huge SEO value)
✅ **Multi-language support** - Works in 20+ languages
✅ **Good documentation** - Comprehensive guides and videos
✅ **Photo/video reviews** - Visual reviews convert 2-3x better than text
✅ **Import reviews** - Can import from other platforms (migration is easy)

**What They Do WRONG:**
❌ **0.8% review conversion rate** - Industry average is 2-10%, they're far below (from actual user complaint)
❌ **Poor support quality** - "Generic and dismissive responses" (verified 1-star review)
❌ **No effort to fix issues** - Users report problems go unresolved for months
❌ **Slow response time** - Days or weeks to respond to support tickets
❌ **Generic email templates** - Same email to everyone (not personalized)
❌ **No AI optimization** - Static emails, no learning, no improvement
❌ **No timing optimization** - Sends at random times (not when customer likely to respond)
❌ **Manual email design required** - No pre-built, high-converting templates

**What They DON'T Solve:**
❌ **WHY review rates are low** - No analysis or suggestions to improve
❌ **How to improve email content** - No A/B testing, no content optimization
❌ **When to send** - No send-time optimization based on customer behavior
❌ **Which customers to target** - No segmentation (asks everyone equally)
❌ **Review quality** - Many reviews are useless ("Great!") with no detail
❌ **Review response** - Merchants get reviews but don't respond (looks bad)
❌ **Multi-platform** - Only works on Shopify (not Etsy, Amazon, etc.)

**Our Opportunity:**

**Automation: Review Request Automator (Enhanced)**
- **Target:** 8-12% review rate (10x better than Judge.me)
- **How:** AI-personalized email content (not templates)
- **When:** Send-time optimization (when customer likely to engage)
- **Who:** Customer segmentation (happy customers vs at-risk)
- **Optimization:** Built-in A/B testing, learns what works
- **Pricing:** £19/month
- **Differentiation:** "Get 10x more reviews than Judge.me"

**Additional Automation: Review Response Automation**
- **Problem:** Merchants get reviews but don't respond (looks neglectful)
- **Solution:** AI auto-responds to reviews (thank yous, addresses concerns professionally)
- **Result:** Every review gets timely, appropriate response
- **Pricing:** £19/month
- **Gap:** NOBODY does this well

- **Solution:** AI detects low-quality reviews, follows up: "Thanks! Could you share what you loved most?"
