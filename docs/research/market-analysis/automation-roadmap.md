# VelocityApps Automation Roadmap (2025-2027)

## Launch (February 2025) - 5 Core Automations

### 1. Pinterest Stock Sync
- **Category:** Inventory Marketing
- **Problem:** Out-of-stock products = lost opportunities (waitlist captures emails)
- **Solution:** Auto-pin OOS products to Pinterest, capture waitlist emails automatically
- **Unique:** ZERO competitors do this (our moat)
- **Pricing:** £19/month
- **Build time:** 1 week
- **Market validation:** Proven model (430 users, £8k MRR from competitor example)
- **Priority:** LAUNCH
- **Differentiation:** "Zero competitors - unique Pinterest waitlist automation"

---

### 2. Review Request Automator (Enhanced)
- **Category:** Reviews & Social Proof
- **Problem:** Judge.me has 0.8% review rate, merchants need 8%+
- **Solution:** AI-personalized review requests, send-time optimization, segmentation
- **Better than:** Judge.me (10x better conversion), Loox (more reliable)
- **Pricing:** £19/month
- **Build time:** 1 week
- **Market size:** HUGE (every store needs reviews)
- **Priority:** LAUNCH
- **Differentiation:** "Get 10x more reviews than Judge.me - 8-12% review rate"

---

### 3. Low Stock Alerts (Predictive)
- **Category:** Inventory Management
- **Problem:** Reactive (discover stockout too late), not multi-channel
- **Solution:** Proactive alerts (predict when you'll run out), multi-channel (Slack/email/SMS)
- **Better than:** Stocky (proactive vs reactive), Back in Stock (alerts not waitlists)
- **Pricing:** £29/month
- **Build time:** 3 days
- **Market size:** Medium (inventory-focused merchants)
- **Priority:** LAUNCH
- **Differentiation:** "Predict stockouts 2 weeks early, not 2 days late"

---

### 4. Abandoned Cart Recovery
- **Category:** Email Marketing
- **Problem:** Klaviyo requires complex flow building (£1,700/month)
- **Solution:** Pre-built 3-email sequence (1hr, 24hr, 72hr), AI-personalized content, one-click activate
- **Better than:** Klaviyo (simpler), Omnisend (cheaper, easier)
- **Pricing:** £29/month
- **Build time:** 1 week
- **Market size:** MASSIVE (70% cart abandonment)
- **Priority:** LAUNCH
- **Differentiation:** "Klaviyo features, 1/60th the setup time - one-click activate"

---

### 5. Best Sellers Auto-Collection
- **Category:** Merchandising
- **Problem:** Manual collection management (tedious, error-prone)
- **Solution:** Auto-creates/updates "Trending" collection based on sales data
- **Better than:** Better Reports (reports don't take action), manual work
- **Pricing:** £19/month
- **Build time:** 3 days
- **Market size:** Medium (merchandising-focused)
- **Priority:** LAUNCH
- **Differentiation:** "Auto-create trending collections - saves 5+ hours/month"

**Launch totals:** 5 automations, 2.5 weeks build time, £19-29/month pricing
