## 🎯 Strategy 1: Freemium Done Right

### Who Does It Well
- **Smile.io** - 50,000+ users (free tier: 500 orders/month)
- **PageFly** - 100,000+ users (free tier: 1 page)
- **Boost Commerce** - 20,000+ users (free tier: under 1,000 products)
- **UpPromote** - 80,000+ users (free tier: under 1,000 orders/month)
- **Stamped.io** - 50,000+ users (free tier: under 500 reviews/month)

### How They Do It

**1. Free Tier is Genuinely Useful**
- ✅ Not crippled (core features included)
- ✅ Realistic limits (500 orders/month covers most small stores)
- ✅ No credit card required (removes friction)
- ✅ Can use forever (not just trial)

**2. Clear Upgrade Path**
- ✅ Visible limits ("43/50 executions used")
- ✅ Upgrade prompts (when approaching limit)
- ✅ Value proposition clear (what you get with upgrade)
- ✅ Easy upgrade (one click, no friction)

**3. Social Proof**
- ✅ "Join 5,000+ free users" on pricing page
- ✅ Testimonials from free users
- ✅ Case studies showing free → paid conversion
- ✅ Community of free users (forums, Facebook groups)

**4. Premium Features Are Obvious Value-Adds**
- ✅ Unlimited (vs limited)
- ✅ Advanced features (vs basic)
- ✅ Priority support (vs community support)
- ✅ White-label (vs branded)

### Why It Works

**Psychology:**
- **Loss aversion** - Once using free tier, don't want to lose it
- **Sunk cost** - Invested time in setup, want to keep using
- **Social proof** - Thousands of free users = trustworthy
- **Value demonstration** - Free tier proves value, upgrade is natural

**Economics:**
- **Low CAC** - Free tier users cost $0 to acquire
- **High conversion** - 5-10% of free users convert to paid
- **Viral growth** - Free users recommend to others
- **Network effects** - More users = more valuable

### Apply to VelocityApps

**Free Tier:**
- 1 automation (any automation)
- 50 executions/month (genuinely useful)
- All core features included (not crippled)
- No credit card required
- Can use forever

**Upgrade Prompts:**
- Visible counter: "43/50 executions used this month"
- Upgrade prompt at 80% usage
- Clear value: "Upgrade to unlimited executions + advanced features"
- One-click upgrade (no friction)

**Social Proof:**
- "Join 5,000+ free users" on pricing page
- Testimonials from free users
- Case studies: "Free user → $10K/month revenue"
- Community: Free users forum

**Premium Features:**
- Unlimited executions (vs 50/month)
- Advanced configuration (vs basic)
- Priority support (vs community)
- Multi-store (vs single store)
- API access (vs dashboard only)

## 🎯 Strategy 2: Deep Platform Integration
