## 📧 Email & SMS Marketing

### Postscript (SMS Marketing)
**Users:** 10,000+ merchants  
**Pricing:** $0.015 per message + $25/mo platform fee  
**Revenue Estimate:** ~$5M ARR  
**Category:** SMS Marketing

#### What They Do Right

**1. SMS-First Approach (Not Email Copycat)**
- ✅ Built specifically for SMS (not email adapted to SMS)
- ✅ Character limits respected (160 chars, not 500)
- ✅ Emoji support (SMS-native)
- ✅ Two-way conversations (merchants can reply)
- ✅ Compliance built-in (TCPA, opt-in/opt-out)

**2. Pre-Built Flows That Work**
- ✅ Welcome series (3-5 messages)
- ✅ Abandoned cart (timed perfectly)
- ✅ Back in stock (instant notifications)
- ✅ Order updates (shipping, delivery)
- ✅ Win-back campaigns (re-engage inactive)

**3. List Growth Tools**
- ✅ Pop-ups (mobile-optimized)
- ✅ Checkout integration (opt-in checkbox)
- ✅ Landing pages (dedicated signup pages)
- ✅ QR codes (in-store signup)
- ✅ Referral incentives (refer friends, get discount)

**4. Revenue Attribution**
- ✅ Tracks revenue per message
- ✅ Shows ROI dashboard ("You sent 1,000 messages, generated $5,000")
- ✅ Conversion tracking (click → purchase)
- ✅ A/B testing results (which message converts better)

**5. Segmentation Based on Shopify Data**
- ✅ High-value customers (spent $500+)
- ✅ VIP customers (purchased 5+ times)
- ✅ Product-specific (bought category X)
- ✅ Location-based (US vs international)
- ✅ Behavior-based (browsed but didn't buy)

#### Key Success Factors

**Why Merchants Choose Postscript:**
1. **Compliance handled automatically** - Merchants don't worry about TCPA violations
2. **Immediate ROI visibility** - See revenue generated per message
3. **Pre-built flows** - Don't need to build from scratch
4. **Two-way conversations** - Customers can reply (not just broadcast)
5. **Usage-based pricing** - Pay for what you use (aligns incentives)

**Best Features:**
1. **Revenue attribution** - Shows exactly how much revenue each message generated
2. **A/B testing** - Test subject lines, timing, content
3. **Smart send times** - AI determines best time to send per customer
4. **Compliance automation** - Handles opt-in/opt-out automatically
5. **Two-way SMS** - Customers reply, merchants respond (conversational)

**User Experience Wins:**
- **Onboarding:** 5-minute setup (connect Shopify, choose flows, done)
- **Dashboard:** Clean, revenue-focused (shows money made, not just messages sent)
- **Templates:** Pre-written messages (just customize, don't write from scratch)
- **Analytics:** Clear metrics (conversion rate, revenue per subscriber, ROI)

**Marketing & Positioning:**
- **Tagline:** "SMS marketing that drives revenue"
- **Value prop:** "See exactly how much revenue each message generates"
- **Differentiation:** "Two-way SMS conversations, not just broadcasts"
- **Social proof:** "10,000+ merchants, $500M+ revenue generated"

**Growth Strategies:**
- **Free trial:** 14 days, 500 messages free
- **Shopify partnership:** Featured in Shopify App Store
- **Content marketing:** Blog about SMS best practices
- **Case studies:** Real revenue numbers from merchants
- **Referral program:** Merchants get credits for referrals

#### What We Can Learn

**For VelocityApps:**
1. **Deep Shopify integration = competitive moat** - Use all available data
2. **Compliance handled automatically** - Merchants don't worry about legal
3. **Usage-based pricing builds trust** - Align incentives (we win when you win)
4. **Show revenue impact** - Make ROI impossible to ignore
5. **Pre-built flows** - Don't make merchants build from scratch

**Implementation Ideas:**
- Show revenue generated per automation in dashboard
- Pre-configured automation templates (just customize)
- Handle all API complexity (merchants see simple interface)
- Compliance built-in (GDPR, CCPA for automations that touch customer data)
