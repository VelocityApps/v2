# Content Marketing Strategy

## Overview

Content marketing drives 30-40% of VelocityApps growth through SEO, thought leadership, and education. This document outlines content types, topics, distribution, and metrics.

**Goal:** 10,000+ monthly blog visitors, 50+ backlinks, Top 10 ranking for 5+ keywords by Year 1

## Content Types

### Blog Posts

**Format:**
- Length: 1,500-2,500 words (long-form SEO)
- Headers: H2, H3 (structure for readability)
- Images: 1 image per 300 words (screenshots, diagrams)
- CTA: In-content + end of post

**SEO Optimization:**
- Target keyword in title (first 60 characters)
- Keyword in first paragraph
- Keyword density: 1-2%
- Internal links (3-5 per post)
- External links (authoritative sources)

**Templates:**

**How-To Guide:**
```markdown
# [Title: How to Do X in Shopify]

## Introduction (150 words)
- Problem statement
- Solution overview
- What you'll learn

## Prerequisites (if any)
- Required tools
- Knowledge needed

## Step-by-Step Guide
### Step 1: [Action]
- Explanation
- Screenshot/diagram
- Common mistakes to avoid

### Step 2: [Action]
...

## Best Practices
- Tip 1
- Tip 2
- Tip 3

## Troubleshooting
- Common issue 1 + solution
- Common issue 2 + solution

## Conclusion
- Recap
- Next steps
- CTA (Try VelocityApps free)
```

**Case Study:**
```markdown
# [Title: How [Store] Achieved X Results]

## Introduction
- Store overview
- Challenge/problem
- Solution (VelocityApps)

## The Challenge
- Problem description
- Impact (time/money lost)

## The Solution
- Automation implemented
- Configuration details
- Timeline

## The Results
- Metrics (before/after)
- Key achievements
- ROI calculation

## Lessons Learned
- What worked
- What didn't
- Recommendations

## Conclusion
- Summary
- CTA (Try VelocityApps)
```

**Secondary (Medium Volume):**

- "how to automate review requests shopify" (170/mo)
- "best shopify automation apps" (140/mo)
- "shopify abandoned cart email automation" (90/mo)

---

### On-Page SEO

**Title Tags:**
- Include target keyword (front-loaded)
- 50-60 characters (truncation)
- Compelling (click-worthy)

**Meta Descriptions:**
- Include target keyword
- 150-160 characters
- Compelling (encourage clicks)

**Headers (H1, H2, H3):**
- H1: Main title (target keyword)
- H2: Section headers (supporting keywords)
- H3: Subsections

**Content:**
- Keyword density: 1-2%
- Natural language (no keyword stuffing)
- Internal links (3-5 per post)
- External links (authoritative sources)

**Images:**
- Alt text (descriptive, keyword-rich)
- File names (descriptive)
- Compression (fast loading)

## Content Promotion

### Launch Strategy

**New Blog Post:**
- Day 1: Publish on blog, share on Twitter/LinkedIn
- Day 2: Share on Reddit (if relevant), engage in comments
- Day 3: Email newsletter (if weekly)
- Week 2: Repost on Medium (if appropriate)

**New Video:**
- Day 1: Upload to YouTube, share on social
- Day 2: Embed in related blog post
- Day 3: Share in email newsletter
- Week 2: Repost on TikTok/Instagram Reels

---

### Repurposing

**Blog Post → Video:**
- Turn how-to guide into tutorial video
- Extract key points for short video

**Video → Blog Post:**
- Transcribe video
- Turn into blog post
- Embed video in post

**Blog Post → Social:**
- Extract quotes for Twitter
- Create infographic for LinkedIn
- Create carousel for Instagram

**Case Study → Multiple Formats:**
- Detailed blog post (2,000+ words)
- Short video testimonial (60 seconds)
- Social media posts (quotes, stats)
- Email campaign (featured story)
